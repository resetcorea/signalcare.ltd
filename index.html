Process

Operating Flow

Operational Data → Digital Evidence → Carbon Asset

SignalCare transforms operational data into verified evidence first. Only after the evidence layer is sealed does the system proceed to AI validation, MRV calculation, certification packages, Carbon Asset registration and Credit Ready preparation.
01
Operational Data
MES, ERP, IoT, CCTV, energy and environmental systems provide production, operation, image, document and sensor records.
INPUT: MES · ERP · IoT · CCTVOUTPUT: Operational Data Set
02
Digital Evidence
Validated records are sealed with SHA-256 hash, digital signature, timestamp, Evidence ID and audit trail.
SEAL: Hash · Signature · TimestampOUTPUT: Evidence Pack
03
AI Validation
AI checks missing values, duplicates, abnormal patterns, time conflicts, unit errors and document consistency.
CHECK: Missing · Outlier · UnitOUTPUT: Trusted Data Layer
04
MRV Calculation
Baseline, additionality, leakage, tCO2e, CH4 and reduction results are calculated using selected methodologies.
CALC: Baseline · tCO2e · CH4OUTPUT: AI MRV Result
05
Certification
ISO, GHG Protocol, CBAM, Verra VCS, Gold Standard, KOC and national MRV packages are prepared.
PACKAGE: ISO · CBAM · RegistryOUTPUT: Verification-ready Docs
06
Carbon Asset
Verified results are organized as Asset ID, Project ID, Evidence ID, Registry ID, Trust Score and Asset Value.
REGISTER: Asset · Project · EvidenceOUTPUT: Carbon Asset Record
07
Credit Ready
Projects become ready for registry integration and verification body review, depending on methodology and approval.
READY: Registry · MethodologyOUTPUT: Issuance Support
08
Settlement
After external approval, sales, settlement, revenue share and verification service records can be managed.
TRACK: Sales · Settlement · ShareOUTPUT: Revenue Record
